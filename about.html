About Our Foundation

Inspiring Change Through Leadership & Compassion

Good Captains of India Foundation is where compassionate leadership meets sustainable action. We are a dedicated nonprofit committed to empowering youth and communities across India through inclusive programs inspired by the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Founded with a vision of dignity, prosperity, and equality for all, we foster compassionate leaders who drive positive change. Our initiatives—youth volunteering, women empowerment workshops, community upliftment, and skill-building projects—create lasting impact, guided by our 5 Ps core values: People, Planet, Prosperity, Peace, and Partnership, aligned with SDGs and aimed for clarity and impact.

Join us in steering India toward a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through compassionate leadership, sustainable development, and inclusive opportunities, steering India toward a brighter, equitable, and thriving future. With the guiding principles of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), we have translated these global aspirations into actionable focus areas. We believe in making a positive impact that resonates for generations to come.

Our Vision

Our vision is a future where every person has access to a life of dignity, prosperity, and equality, and where our planet thrives in harmony with the needs of its people.

Our Leadership

Guiding Leadership with Purpose and Integrity

At Good Captains of India Foundation, our leadership is driven by a deep commitment to understanding the needs of communities. By fostering collaboration with partners, volunteers, and stakeholders, we create impactful initiatives that advance education, sustainability, social welfare, and inclusive growth.

Capt. Parthiban Sivaraj

Founder & Managing Trustee

Capt. Parthiban Sivaraj, Retired Captain from Air India, brings decades of distinguished leadership from the skies to ground-level impact. A veteran aviator with an illustrious career piloting for one of India's premier airlines, he navigated challenges with precision, safety, and unwavering commitment—skills he now channels into social good.

As Founder and Managing Trustee of Good Captains of India Foundation, Capt. Parthiban steers our mission to empower youth and communities through SDG-aligned programs. His vision of compassionate leadership and sustainable action inspires initiatives like women empowerment workshops and community upliftment projects.With a heart for service, he leads by example, fostering resilient leaders for India's brighter future.

Nizamudeen Yunus

Trustee

Nizamudeen Yunus is a dynamic Founder-Director of Veni Vidi Vici School of Training, Certified Counselling Psychologist, Researcher, Writer, Thought Leader, Professional Orator, Motivational Speaker, Career Coach, and Life-Skills Trainer. With a Bachelor’s in Psychology, Master’s in Counselling Psychology, and Mentoria Advanced Career Coach certification, he brings 13+ years of training diverse groups—from adolescents to seniors, slums to Rotary/Lions clubs—and 15+ years in public speaking.

His achievements include 35 awards, 1,000+ training programs impacting over 1 lakh people, leadership as Opposition Leader at Yuva Parliament 2017 (IIM-Rohtak), Tamil Nadu Youth Delegate for Pravasi Bharatiya Divas (2017), and top scorer in Communicate to Change (C2C). He has authored 6 research papers in the International Journal of Social Studies and championed women empowerment through trainings.

Currently President of World Youth Federation (2025–present) and Director of Business Development & Public Relations at IntecAi Digital Pvt Ltd (2024–present), with past roles including President of United Psychology Association (2020–2025) and Leo Club of Chennaipattinam (2016–2018). Nizamudeen’s passion for transformative leadership strengthens Good Captains of India Foundation’s mission.
